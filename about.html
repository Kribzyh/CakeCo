About

Cake Co.

Founded in 2020, Cake Co. is a proudly homegrown cake shop dedicated to creating beautifully crafted cakes that bring joy to every occasion. What started as a small kitchen passion project during the quarantine days quickly blossomed into a full-fledged business loved by locals and sweet tooths alike.


Specializing exclusively in cakes, we focus on quality, flavor, and creative presentation — no distractions, just pure cake goodness. From classic favorites to dreamy custom creations, each cake is made with the finest ingredients and a whole lot of love.

Over the years, our signature Strawberry Cake has become a fan favorite — known for its fluffy sponge layers, fresh strawberries, and silky whipped cream frosting. It's the kind of cake that gets requested again and again. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, milestone, or just need a slice of happiness, Cake Co. is here to make it extra special — one delicious cake at a time.
